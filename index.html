About Tri Star

Hardworking. Local. Dependable.

What We Do

Our Services

From routine lawn maintenance to complete stump removal, we offer everything your property needs to look its best — all year long.

Lawn Mowing & Routine Care

Consistent, reliable mowing, edging, and blowing to keep your lawn looking sharp week after week. We work around your schedule and treat your property with respect.

Landscaping

Transform your outdoor space with custom landscaping solutions. From new garden bed installations to complete yard redesigns, we bring your vision to life with quality plants and materials.

Mulching

Fresh mulch installation around trees, flower beds, and garden borders. We prep the area, lay a clean edge, and spread premium mulch to protect your plants and boost curb appeal.

Hedge Trimming

Precise hedge and shrub trimming to keep your property looking neat and well-maintained. We shape bushes, trim overgrowth, and clean up all clippings when the job is done.

Seasonal Cleanups

Spring and fall cleanups to get your yard ready for the season ahead. We remove leaves, cut back dead growth, clear debris, and leave your property looking fresh and tidy.

Stump Grinding & Removal

Got an old stump taking up space in your yard? We grind it down below the surface, clean up the debris, and leave the area ready for grass, mulch, or whatever you have planned next.

Not sure what you need? Give us a call — we're happy to walk through your yard and give you an honest, no-pressure estimate.
